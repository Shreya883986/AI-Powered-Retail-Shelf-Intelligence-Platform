# Business Insights

## Revenue Analysis

### Key Findings

- Revenue varies significantly across stores.
- High-performing stores contribute a large share of overall sales.

### Recommendation

Focus inventory allocation on top-performing stores.

---

## Product Performance

### Key Findings

- Certain products generate significantly higher revenue.
- Low-performing products occupy shelf space without contributing substantially to sales.

### Recommendation

Optimize shelf space allocation based on product performance.

---

## Inventory Analysis

### Key Findings

- Inventory mismatches exist between expected and actual stock.
- Some products experience repeated stock shortages.

### Recommendation

Improve replenishment planning.

---

## Shelf Intelligence Analysis

### Key Findings

- Products with lower stock levels often experience reduced sales opportunities.
- Shelf availability directly impacts sales performance.

### Recommendation

Implement automated shelf monitoring systems.

---

## Demand Forecasting

### Key Findings

- Historical sales trends can predict future demand patterns.

### Recommendation

Use predictive analytics to optimize inventory planning.

---

## Overall Business Impact

The AI-Powered Retail Shelf Intelligence Platform enables:

- Better inventory control
- Reduced stock-outs
- Improved forecasting
- Increased operational efficiency
- Data-driven decision making
